# Business-Performance-Customer-Insights-Dashboard (Excel)
This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance and customer metrics. It tracks key KPIs including sales, profit, and customer growth against targets, helping to monitor business health over time

## Project Overview
This Excel-based dashboard analyzes sales, profit, customer growth, and regional performance using structured datasets and dynamic visuals. It supports KPI tracking, target comparison, and insight generation for business decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement
Businesses often struggle to monitor and evaluate their sales performance, customer growth, and regional market trends in a unified, actionable way. Without a clear view of key metrics, decision-makers cannot quickly identify underperforming regions, understand customer behavior, or optimize strategies to meet targets. This gap can result in missed opportunities, inconsistent revenue growth, and suboptimal customer experiences. 

---

## Objectives
- Track key business performance indicators
- Compare monthly sales against targets
- Analyze regional and geographic sales distribution
- Monitor customer growth trends
- Evaluate customer experience across service dimensions
---

## Data Used
- **Sales Data:** Monthly sales, profit, region, and targets  
- **Customer Data:** Customer count and growth by month  
- **Customer Feedback Data:** Ratings across service categories  
- **Geographical Data:** Country-level sales figures  

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Customer Count  
- Sales vs Target (%)  

---

## Dashboard Features
- Monthly sales trend vs target  
- Regional sales distribution  
- Geographic sales performance map  
- Monthly customer growth analysis  
- Customer experience evaluation  
- KPI summary cards  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales performance varies significantly by region, indicating uneven market penetration.  
- Monthly sales do not consistently meet targets, suggesting seasonality or execution gaps.  
- Customer growth shows a positive overall trend but fluctuates across months.  
- Customer experience scores differ across service dimensions.  
- Sales activity is concentrated in specific geographic locations.

---

## Recommendations
- Prioritize underperforming regions with targeted sales initiatives.  
- Adjust sales targets based on historical and seasonal trends.  
- Replicate strategies used during high customer growth periods.  
- Improve low-scoring customer experience areas to boost retention.  
- Explore expansion opportunities in underrepresented markets.

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables and Charts  
- Excel formulas and calculations  
- Dashboard layout and design techniques
